Doctor Profile

Dr. Mona Shroff

Senior IVF Consultant and Reproductive Medicine Specialist, National Strategic Director, and Senior Clinical Director at Nova IVF Fertility.

Profile summary

Focused on IVF outcomes, ethical care, and difficult fertility journeys.

Dr. Mona works with couples facing infertility, repeated IVF failure, recurrent implantation failure, endometriosis-associated infertility, donor-programme decisions, and age-related fertility concerns. Her approach blends structured clinical planning with emotionally safe counselling.

  • MBBS, MD (Obstetrics & Gynaecology)
  • 25+ years of clinical experience, including 23+ years in IVF and reproductive medicine
  • Ex-Professor, SMIMER Medical College, Surat
  • Mentor, trainer, author, and national fertility leader
