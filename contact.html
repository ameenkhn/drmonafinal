Before your appointment

Bring the information that helps the consult move faster.

  • Previous IVF summaries, stimulation sheets, embryo reports, and transfer details
  • Hormone tests, AMH, ultrasound findings, laparoscopy or hysteroscopy reports if available
  • Semen analysis and related male-factor reports
  • A short list of your biggest concerns or questions so the call stays focused

Second-opinion consults are most useful when previous treatment documents are available in advance.

Availability

Simple answers to common contact questions

Are international consultations available?

Yes. International patients can book online consultations by prior appointment and receive treatment-planning support remotely.

How long are appointments?

Online consultations are typically 20 to 30 minutes. Second-opinion case reviews are usually 45 to 60 minutes.

What is included?

History review, clinical interpretation, practical next steps, and in case-review appointments, a more detailed structured recommendation.
